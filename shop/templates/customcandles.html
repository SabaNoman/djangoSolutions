CUSTOM CANDLES

We love making custom candles and we specialize in producing custom candles for events, retail stores, corporate gifts, companies, day spas, hotels, brands, and fashion labels.

The entire process is customized to get the perfect candles to suit your needs. We work with you each step of the way! The Candle Library has a strong focus on using premium products that are eco-friendly. All of our candles are made using 100% eco Soy Wax. We use lead-free, cotton wicks, and high-quality premium fragrance oils that are Australian made. Candles are produced in small batches, and each candle is individually poured by hand.
